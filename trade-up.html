Anniversaries, milestones, the upgraded centre stone you've always wanted — when the moment comes, your original Lovelri ring is worth its full original price toward whatever's next.

How it works

  1. Bring back any Lovelri ring — engagement, wedding band, anniversary. Original receipt or our records both work.
  2. We credit 100% of what you paid toward a new piece of at least 2× the original value. (Updated terms — see box below.)
  3. Your old ring is responsibly recycled — the metal goes to refining, the centre stone is recut, regraded, and finds a new home.
  4. You walk out with the upgrade, fully appraised, with a fresh certificate and warranty.

Why we offer this

Two reasons. First, an engagement ring should be the start of a relationship with your jeweller — not a transaction that ends at the till. Second, a lab-grown diamond holds its sparkle and structural integrity forever, but as production technology improves, larger stones become more accessible. We'd rather be the place you upgrade with than lose you to a competitor on a milestone purchase.

Common questions

Can I trade up to a piece outside Lovelri's collection?

Yes — the trade-up credit applies to any custom design Tony makes for you, not just the catalogue.

What if my ring is damaged?

Normal wear is covered — bent prongs, surface scratches, even a small chipped diamond. Severe damage (deep gouges, missing major stones from non-Lovelri causes) may reduce the credit; we'll assess in person.

What if I bought elsewhere but want to trade in?

We'll appraise any ring you bring in. The credit won't be 100% (that's reserved for our own pieces) but we'll make a fair offer based on current market value.

Is there a time limit?

No. Five years, twenty years — same terms. We mean lifetime.
