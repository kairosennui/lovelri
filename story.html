What we believe

Three things, every time.

01

Honesty over upsell.

Lab and natural diamonds, side by side, both with full grading reports. We tell you when a smaller stone in better cut beats a larger one with poor proportions. Even when it's not the more expensive recommendation.

02

Made by hand, in-house.

Every piece is set, polished, and finished at our Bay Street bench. No outsourcing to overseas workshops. When you see a Lovelri ring, Tony has held it.

03

A relationship, not a sale.

Free annual cleaning. Lifetime trade-up. The customer who buys a $3,500 first ring and the customer who buys a $30,000 anniversary piece are treated identically — because often, ten years apart, they're the same person.
